Shree Venkateshwara College of Physiotherapy

Our mission is to adopt progressive educational practices that inspire quality learning, encourage independent thought, and nurture the all-round growth of every student, enabling them to serve society and the nation with excellence.

  • Shree Venkateshwara College of Physiotherapy has been a pioneer in the field of education since 2018, offering quality undergraduate programs.
  • The college features well-furnished, spacious, and ventilated classrooms equipped with advanced audio-visual aids.
  • We have state-of-the-art laboratories for Anatomy & Physiology, Exercise Therapy, Electrotherapy, Walking School, and an advanced gymnasium.
  • The library houses a collection of over 4,000 books along with subscriptions to national and international journals.
  • Our separate digital library provides access to a wide range of digital resources and online journals in Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation.
  • We also have a well-equipped outpatient department that offers treatment completely free of cost.

Prof. Nandakumar Ramasamy, MPT Ortho (PhD).

Principal

Warm greetings to everyone! Welcome to the portal of Shree Venkateshwara College of Paramedical Sciences (College of Physiotherapy). I hope the time you spend here will be enriching and rewarding. It has been a privilege to serve this wonderful institution as the Founding Principal since 2018.

  • Healthcare is a noble profession, held in the highest regard since ancient times.
  • Physiotherapy, as a vital branch of medical science, offers opportunities and possibilities beyond imagination.
  • The unique blend of medical science and physical movement aids in rehabilitation and promotes harmony of mind, body, and soul.
  • Our ultimate goal is to prepare physiotherapists who are excellent clinical thinkers, dedicated team players, and lifelong learners capable of serving across diverse healthcare domains.
  • We take pride in our highly qualified faculty — exceptional teachers and skilled practitioners whose contributions continue to inspire growth in the field of physiotherapy.

We warmly welcome all aspiring students to our institution for a promising new beginning. Happy learning and happy surfing!
