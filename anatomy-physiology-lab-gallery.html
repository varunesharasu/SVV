Our student seminars are designed to enhance the learning experience through interactive knowledge sharing sessions. These events provide a platform for students to present their research, share clinical experiences, and engage in meaningful discussions with peers and faculty members. The seminars cover various aspects of physiotherapy, including recent advances, case studies, and evidence-based practices.
