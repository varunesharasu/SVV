Academic Programmes

Shree Venkateshwara College of Physiotherapy offers the Bachelor of Physiotherapy (B.P.T) program, designed to prepare students for successful careers in physiotherapy and rehabilitation sciences.

PROGRAMS: BACHELOR OF PHYSIOTHERAPY(B.P.T)

Program Overview

Physiotherapy is a healthcare profession to help people affected by injury, illness or disability through movement, exercise, manual therapy, physical agents, education and advice.

It provides services to individuals and populations to develop, maintain and restore maximum movement and functional ability throughout the lifespan.

Intake & Eligibility
Intake: 100 Seats
Eligibility: HSC with Physics / Chemistry / Biology
