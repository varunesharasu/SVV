Job Types & Roles

Explore diverse career paths and professional roles in physiotherapy

Clinical Roles

Direct patient care and treatment

Assistant Physiotherapist

Support senior physiotherapists in patient treatment and rehabilitation programs

Osteopath

Specialize in musculoskeletal treatment through manual therapy and manipulation

Sports Physio Rehabilitator

Work with athletes to prevent, diagnose, and treat sports-related injuries

Management & Leadership

Administrative and leadership positions

Therapy Manager

Oversee therapy departments, manage staff, and ensure quality patient care

Private Practitioner

Establish and run your own physiotherapy clinic or practice

Education and research opportunities

Lecturer

Researcher

Research Assistant

Handle patient appointments, inquiries, and provide administrative support
