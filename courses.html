Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT)

A 4½ year undergraduate programme including 12 months of compulsory internship, affiliated to The Tamilnadu Dr.MGR Medical University, Chennai.

Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT)

4.5 Years 2025-2026 Degree Programme

Physiotherapy is a dynamic healthcare profession that promotes optimal health and function. Physiotherapists work with individuals of all ages to prevent, diagnose, and treat movement dysfunctions and enhance physical capabilities.

Our BPT programme combines theoretical knowledge with extensive practical training, preparing students for diverse career opportunities in hospitals, rehabilitation centers, sports organizations, and private practice.

Key Learning Areas:
  • Anatomy and Physiology
  • Biomechanics and Kinesiology
  • Exercise Therapy
  • Electrotherapy
  • Orthopedic and Neurological Conditions
  • Cardiorespiratory Physiotherapy
  • Sports Physiotherapy
  • Community Based Rehabilitation

Curriculum Overview

Our comprehensive curriculum is designed to provide strong foundation in physiotherapy education.

First Year

  • Human Anatomy
  • Human Physiology
  • Basic Nursing & First Aid
  • Biochemistry
  • English
  • Introduction to Physiotherapy

Second Year

  • Pathology & Microbiology
  • Pharmacology
  • Exercise Therapy
  • Electrotherapy
  • Biomechanics

Third Year

  • Orthopedics
  • Neurology
  • Surgery
  • Medicine
  • General Psychology
  • Clinical Observation

Fourth Year

  • Orthopedic Physiotherapy
  • Neurological Physiotherapy
  • Cardiorespiratory Physiotherapy
  • Sports Physiotherapy
  • Community Based Rehabilitation
  • Research Methodology

Internship (12 Months)

Compulsory rotatory internship in various departments including Orthopedics, Neurology, Cardiopulmonary, Sports Medicine, Pediatrics, and Community Based Rehabilitation. Students gain hands-on clinical experience under the supervision of experienced physiotherapists.

Featured Program

Our most sought-after academic offering

Master of Physiotherapy (MPT)

2 Years 60 Intakes Degree Programme

A Master of Physiotherapy (MPT) is a 2-year postgraduate degree for advanced physical therapy skills, focusing on areas like Orthopaedics, Neurology, Sports, or Pediatrics, to prepare specialists for complex roles in hospitals, rehab centers, and research, building on a Bachelor's in Physiotherapy (BPT) to offer deeper expertise in treating movement disorders and improving function.

Our MPT programme combines theoretical knowledge with extensive practical training, preparing students for diverse career opportunities in hospitals, rehabilitation centers, sports organizations, and private practice.

Key Learning Areas:
  • Anatomy and Physiology
  • Biomechanics and Kinesiology
  • Exercise Therapy
  • Electrotherapy
  • Orthopedic and Neurological Conditions
  • Cardiorespiratory Physiotherapy
  • Sports Physiotherapy
