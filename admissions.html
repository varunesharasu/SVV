Admissions

Join Shree Venkateshwara College of Physiotherapy and embark on a rewarding career in healthcare. Our admission process is simple, transparent, and designed to welcome aspiring students.

Eligibility Criteria

Before applying, ensure you meet the following eligibility requirements for admission to our BPT programme.

Educational Qualification

Candidates must have passed Higher Secondary Examination (10+2) with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Botany and Zoology as main subjects.

Minimum Marks

Minimum aggregate of 50% marks in Science subjects (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) for general category. Relaxation applicable for reserved categories as per government norms.

Age Limit

Candidates should have completed 17 years of age at the time of admission. There is no upper age limit for admission to the BPT programme.

Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to secure your admission at Shree Venkateshwara College of Physiotherapy.

1

Online Application

Fill out the online application form with accurate details and submit required documents.

2

Document Verification

Submit original documents for verification at the college office or via online upload.

3

Counseling

Attend counseling session as per schedule and select your preferred course.

4

Fee Payment

Pay the admission fee to confirm your seat and collect your admission order.

Required Documents

Please ensure you have the following documents ready for the admission process.

  • 10th Mark Sheet and Certificate
  • 12th Mark Sheet and Certificate
  • Transfer Certificate from previous institution
  • Community Certificate (for reserved categories)
  • Nativity Certificate
  • Passport size photographs (4 copies)
  • Aadhaar Card copy
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable)
