Research Areas

Our key focus areas for research and innovation in physiotherapy.

Musculoskeletal

Research on orthopedic conditions, sports injuries, and rehabilitation protocols.

Neurological

Studies on stroke rehabilitation, Parkinson's disease, and neurological disorders.

Cardiorespiratory

Research on cardiac and pulmonary rehabilitation techniques and outcomes.

Pediatrics

Studies on developmental disorders and pediatric physiotherapy interventions.

Recent Publications

Selected publications by our faculty and students in reputed journals.

International Journal
Efficacy of Manual Therapy in Chronic Low Back Pain: A Randomized Controlled Trial
Dr. S. Kumar, et al.
Journal of Manual Therapy, 2025

This study evaluates the effectiveness of manual therapy techniques in patients with chronic low back pain.

National Journal
Effectiveness of Core Strengthening in Post-Stroke Patients: A Comparative Study
Dr. R. Venkat, et al.
Indian Journal of Physiotherapy, 2025

Comparative analysis of core strengthening exercises in improving balance and gait in stroke patients.

International Journal
Role of Tele-rehabilitation in Post-COVID Pulmonary Rehabilitation
Dr. M. Priya, et al.
Journal of Telemedicine, 2024

Study on the effectiveness of telerehabilitation in pulmonary rehabilitation of post-COVID patients.

Conference Paper
Kinesiotaping vs. Traditional Taping in Ankle Sprain: A Clinical Trial
Final Year Students
National Physiotherapy Conference 2025

Comparative study presented at the national conference on sports physiotherapy.

Prevalence of Musculoskeletal Disorders Among Computer Professionals
Dr. A. Rajan, et al.
Journal of Occupational Health, 2024

Epidemiological study on work-related musculoskeletal disorders in IT professionals.
